Energy Usage:
To calculate your energy consumption, check your electricity bill to find the total kilowatt-hour consumption and then divide the total consumption by the number of people in your household. i
Water Usage:
To calculate your water consumption, check your water bill to find the total amount of water consumed (in cubic meters) and then divide the total consumption by the number of people in your household. i
Waste Management:
To calculate your waste, estimate your household weekly waste then divide it by 7 and the number of people in your household. Average weekly waste production per person: Low 1 kg, Moderate 2 kg, High 3 kg. i
Diet:
To calculate your meat consumption, estimate your daily meat consumption in kilograms. Average daily meat consumption: Low (vegetarian/vegan) 0 kg, Moderate 0.033 kg, High 0.067 kg. i
